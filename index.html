Tiny Ticks Quanta 600 FEG ESEM

These are microscopic images taken of ticks using the Quanta 600 FEG Environmental Scanning Electron Microscope in the Nanoscale Characterization Facility (NCF) at the Krishna P. Singh Center for Nanotechnology.

These ticks were gathered in late May 2025.

Thank you to the Nanoscale Characterization Facility (NCF) at the Singh Center for Nanotechnology! https://github.com/ericoc/tiny-ticks
